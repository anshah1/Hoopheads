How to Play

Guess the NBA Player within 8 tries
Divisions
  • Red = wrong conference
  • Yellow = right conference, wrong division
  • Green = right division
Heights & Ages
  • Yellow = within 2 inches/years
  • Green = correct
Examples

The correct player is not in the Western Conference, is slightly shorter than 6'6", and is younger than 27.

The correct player is in the Southwest Division and is slighty taller than 6'7". They are older than 22.

The correct player is in either the Central or Atlantic division, shorter than 7'0", and slightly older than 23.

All players who played at least 30 games in 2024-2025 are able to be guessed

Only players who averaged at least 7 points per game will be the answer
